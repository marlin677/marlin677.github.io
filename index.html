Safety & District Facts

Smoke Alarm Tip

You need a working smoke alarm in every bedroom, living area, and hallway. Check your batteries every month.

Response Readiness

A house fire can become deadly in under 3 minutes. Get out, stay out, call 111.

Escape Plans

In a fire, you have less than 3 minutes to get out. Always identify two ways out of every room in your home.
