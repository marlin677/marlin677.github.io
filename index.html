Safety & District Facts

Smoke Alarm Tip

You need a working smoke alarm in every bedroom, living area, and hallway. Check your batteries every month.

Response Readiness

Christchurch District maintains high operational readiness. Ensure your gear is checked and stowed correctly before every shift.

Escape Plans

In a fire, you have less than 3 minutes to get out. Always identify two ways out of every room in your station and home.
