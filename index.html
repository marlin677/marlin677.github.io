Smoke Alarms Save Lives

IT'S THE ONLY WAY TO WAKE UP TO FIRE.

You can't smell smoke when you're asleep. Working smoke alarms are your only voice when you're dreaming. Ensure you have working alarms in every bedroom, hallway, and living area. Test them every month.
