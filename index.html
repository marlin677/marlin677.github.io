Smoke Alarms Save Lives

WORKING SMOKE ALARMS ARE THE ONLY WAY TO WAKE UP TO FIRE.

You can't smell smoke when you're asleep. Ensure you have working smoke alarms in every bedroom, hallway, and living area. Test them every month.
